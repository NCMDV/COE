Our Products

Note: The Base Premium indicated on this page is the minimum base premium for each Insurance Policy. Total Annual Premium will still be adjusted based on the following factors:
1. Age: Age is one of the most significant factors affecting the base premium. Generally, premiums increase with age as the risk of mortality rises.
2. Gender: Gender also plays a role, with females typically having lower base premiums than males due to their longer life expectancy.
3. Smoking status: Smokers face significantly higher base premiums due to the increased health risks associated with their habit.
4. Occupation: Certain high-risk occupations may also lead to higher base premiums.
5. Medical History: Your medical history, including past surgeries, hospitalizations, and treatments, is also considered. A history of serious illnesses or medical interventions can lead to higher premiums.
